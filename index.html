dataset.tools is a desktop file manager

Data validation

Not sure your data is ready for prime time? You can validate your CSV on the fly using CSVlint, so you can be sure your data is reuse ready before you publish it!

Export your changes back to data.world

Once you've finished editing your dataset, dataset.tools lets you sync your changes with your data.world profile!

Publish on Github

Want to edit your CSV quickly and easily? Download dataset.tools along with the companion tool: dataset.chrome and clean away!!
